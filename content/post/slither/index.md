# Slither

[![&#x56FE;&#x7247;alt](https://i1.wp.com/1.bp.blogspot.com/-w5GMADRXlX8/XbMwKQf3_DI/AAAAAAAADHU/LHFA0pehinAN1090Qom38xiq2KbaemO5wCLcBGAsYHQ/s1600/logo.png?w=640&ssl=1)](https://github.com/crytic/slither)
